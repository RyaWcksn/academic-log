$$\begin{bmatrix}
a & 3 \\
-1 & a+b
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
4 & d-2c \\
d+2c & -2
\end{bmatrix}$$

Answer :

$$
a = 4
$$

$$
a + b = -2 \\
4 + b = -2 \\
b = -2 -4 \\
b = -6
$$


$$
d-2c = 3 \\
d+2c = -1 \\
\cancel{-4} c = \cancel{4} \\
c = -1
$$

$$
d-2c = 3 \\
d+(-2) = -1 \\
d+2 = 3 \\
d = 3 - 2 \\
d = 1
$$

$a = 4, b = -6, c = -1, d = 1$
